Customer Lifecycle Value


Customers are the bedrock for any business.

A strong relationship between customer and company is built over time. Several factors contributing to strengthening of relationship with customers include quality of product or service, efficient supply chain & business operations processes management, effective customer management approach resolving every customer query.

Strong relationship with customers contributes to longer association and increased customer retention and higher Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) can be considered the value which a customer contributes to a business over their period of engagement with the brand – beginning with a customer’s first purchase and finally finishing with what is referred to as the ‘churn.’

While most businesses spend millions of dollars through marketing initiatives to attract new customers, they spend just a fraction on managing their existing customer.

Success rate of selling to existing customer is 60% to 70% whereas selling to a new customer is between 5 to 20%

Designing the right Customer Relationship Management (CRM) process and using the right applications and software for business you can enable customer retention which means higher CLV. The advantages of utilizing Customer Lifetime Value as a focus includes delivering better customer relationships.

Consumers who continually return to your brand will eventually spend more with you and will generate more revenue for you. Satisfied customers also help grow your business by recommending your brand to their friends & family.

Experts say that word of mouth is the strongest advertising for any company. When you build a great customer experience, customers will tell each other about that. Word of mouth is very powerful
